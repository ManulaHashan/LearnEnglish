NOUN

A noun is a part of speech that names a person, place, thing, idea, action or quality

There are 5 types of nouns

  • Common Noun
  • Proper Noun
  • Abstract Noun
  • Possessive Noun
  • Collective Noun

Examples

cat, sock, ship, hero, monkey, baby, match

  • The sun shines
  • Anna goes to school

PRONOUN

A pronoun is a word that replaces a noun in a sentence. Pronouns are used so that our language is not cumbersome with the same nouns being repeated over and over in a paragraph.

Examples

I, me, mine, myself, she, her, hers, herself, we, us, ours and ourselves

John is hungry. He wants to eat

John is the Noun and He reffers as pronoun
