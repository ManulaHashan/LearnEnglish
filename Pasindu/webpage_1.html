PARTS OF A SPEECH

There are eight parts of speech in the English language:
noun, pronoun, verb, adjective, adverb, preposition,conjunctions and interjections
The part of speech indicates how the word functions in meaning as well as grammatically within the sentence.

NOUNS


Definition:People,Places,Things


Examples

  • dog
  • cat
  • town
  • manila

The sun shines.
Anna goes to school.
